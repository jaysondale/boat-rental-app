About us

Kennisis Lake Marina is the heart of the lake. It's a place for cottagers to come and stop by and get an ice cream cone with their family, or fill up their boats with gas to enjoy a fun-filled day on the lake. If you're renting a cottage on a lake, we provide boat rentals to make your vacation up north that much more enjoyable. We also offer marine services. If your boat breaks down or needs repairs, we've got you covered, and there's no hassle of having to take it too far from the lake.
