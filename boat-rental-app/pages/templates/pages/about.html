Kennisis Lake Marina & Storage

Your Cottaging Community

Kennisis Lake marina is owned and operated by a group of passionate cottagers and local residents who strive to provide high quality, friendly service to all customers. We aim to be the community center for cottagers and local residents on Kennisis Lake and the surrounding area by offering amenities and social events throughout the year!

  • General Store
